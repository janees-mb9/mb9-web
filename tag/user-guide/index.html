8 Ways to make your story postings remarkable in HumSurfer.com

Sunday, April 26th, 2009
  1. Title is the Key. Good post always come up with good Title. Readers have the tendency to click on the story when the title is meaningful. So, do ensure to have a descriptive and catchy title of 5 to 10 words in length
  2. Do not write the title all in CAPS, which sounds shouting at readers. The maximum length of title is 80 characters, so you have to be creative to include as much message as you can within this limit.
  3. Never paste URLs and HTML stuff in the Title as this is annoying to the users. Also, such titles are get flagged easily by users causing the story to be ineligible to reach hot page.
  4. Do not write keywords repetitively in the Title. Users will not prefer to read those stories and may get flagged by HumSurfer community. So, generate some interest in the story with sweet Title and move on to the description.
  5. Write a short summary of what you found interesting in the article. This is the place where you tell other HumSurfer’s about your opinion.
    • Do not just copy paste from the article.
    • But, to support your opinion, do feel free to quote an interesting sentence or two from the article.
  8. Stories without opinions becomes impersonal and doesn’t appeal to the community and are hard to attract conversations from HumSurfer’s.
  9. “If a picture is worth a thousand words, a video is a worth of million”. HumSurfer will take the images and videos from the destination link of the article and display it for your selection (This part comes in step 3). Do include relevant images/videos in your post.
