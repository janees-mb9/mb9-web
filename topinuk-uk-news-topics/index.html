TopInUK - UK News in topics

TopInUK helps you keep track of news from UK. The app aggregates and groups together news from various newspapers in real-time. The app is available as a free app on the Apple Appstore.

App Description

TopInUK gathers and organizes news from various UK based newspapers in real-time. It groups news into topics and gives you a concise view of what is happening across UK. By using TopInUK iPhone app, you are able to easily keep track of the news and trends collected for you right from your iPhone.
