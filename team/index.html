Management Team

Kok Young Chai - CEO

Kok Young is an astute technology and business executive leader with over 17 years of experience in leveraging and selling technology to drive efficiency and business goals. He had held various senior positions across different organizations and had also worked across Asia Pacific countries, specifically spending 2 years in China.


He was most recently Senior Vice President of Trusted Source Pte Ltd, the IT division and wholly owned subsidiary of Temasek. He led the entire IT Applications group, overseeing the daily systems operations, new projects implementation and IT strategies for the organization.


Kok Young started his career in the public sector under the Ministry of Home Affairs Singapore, focusing in IT security. He then moved on to a dot.com company in year 2000 as the CTO, leading a team to build B2B E-Commerce platform for the healthcare industry.


Prior to his last job, he was with IMS Health, a world’s leading healthcare information services provider. There he was the Director of IT, Development and Operations, across 16 APAC countries delivering business intelligence insights to his clients. In his 8 years, he had done multiple business process optimization and outsourcing, saving the organization millions of dollars per year


Kok Young holds B.Eng (Hons) in Mechanical Engineering from National University of Singapore

Sandeep Chellappen - Head of Projects

Sandeep Chellappen has over 10 years experience in software design and development. He specializes in data mining and analysis. His experience includes writing web crawlers and algorithms for data analysis.


Sandeep holds B.Tech in EEE from National Institute of Technology, Calicut

Shameem Othayoth - Principal Architect, Mobile Platforms

Shameem Othayoth has over 10 years of experience in design and development of application on various technologies ranging web 2.0 application to embedded system. He has expertise in algorithm design and optimization. He manages various web properties of MB9 and is an expert in cloud solution.
