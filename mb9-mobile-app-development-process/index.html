MB9 Mobile App Development Process

Understanding Requirements. Intuitive design. Smooth execution

Requirements and Business goal Evaluation

We engage our customers to understand requirements. It involves sharing ideas and refining the concept. Whether customers want to develop a free app or paid app, we work with them to understand their business goals and suggest features and designs to maximize their app potential. We bring value to our customers by enhancing their business success.

User Experience Design

Having understood the business goals, we come up with designs to suite to the app audience taste. Our designs are always focused towards enhancing customer retention, branding, lead generation, and sales. This process involves sharing app wireframes for feedback discussions, developing the designs and finally the hotspots where the customers can review the design and flow.

App Development

Once the App design and flow are reviewed, we get on to the task for developing the app. Be it native app or an interactive app which requires server side components, our experts make sure the application quality and responsiveness are of highest standards. Our experts specialize on iPhone and Android apps and server side technologies like php, ruby and .net.

Testing and Feedback

We follow the agile process for app development, which involves frequent releases and customer feedback during the development process. At onset of development, milestones are defined and at each milestone the app is reviewed by customer. This keeps our customers informed all through the process.

App submission and Launch

Our service involves application submission and making sure it gets launched on the app store. During the launch process, we guide our customers for creating maximum visibility and PR. We provide support to develop marketing campaigns well suited for App.

Ongoing Support

App launch is the first stage in creating successful App. The audience feedback will get you to the next stage , towards greater success. We consider ourself as partners to customer success, and provide continuous support for updates and feature enhancements.
