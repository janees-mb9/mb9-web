InfoTrie - Brand Solutions

InfoTrie - Brand Solutions track how your brand is performing on the internet and social media.

Benefits

  • Brand Perception

    Keep track of how your brand is perceived.

  • Campaign Effectiveness

    Know and measure how your advertising and marketing campaigns are working.

  • Turn Social Media to your Advantage

How it Works

InfoTrie automatically does content aggregation from News Sources, Blogs and Social Networks. It arranges the Contextually Related Articles, Images and Videos into Topics. InfoTrie - Brand Solutions use this to do analysis on how your brand is performing and translates this into meaningful metrics.
