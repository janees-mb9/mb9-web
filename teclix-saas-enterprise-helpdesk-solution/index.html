Teclix - SaaS Enterprise Helpdesk Solution

Teclix is an Enterprise Helpdesk Solution, powered by Microsoft Azure. This is a SaaS solution and can be used either as a customer facing Technical Support Helpdesk solution or as a Helpdesk Tool for your internal organization. We provide integration with Google Apps and Windows Live to enable you to get started quickly. AD integration will be available soon.

Benefits

  1. Pay as you go, with monthly billing
  2. Microsoft Live and Google Apps integrated - enables you to deploy the solution seamlessly with your existing authentication schemes.
  3. Fully configurable workflow.
