Software Development

A successful outsourcing relationship is based on clear communication and transparency of action. MB9 can work as Software Development outsourcing partner of your company and we can your product development at our India offices. An product or application can be created from the ground up, providing you with exactly what you need, not just what is available.

We offer world-class software development services for Semantic Solutions, Web Applications, Cloud Applications, Enterprise and Business Applications. We have our teams working on solutions across several industries including Media, Travel, Health-care, Automobile, Education, and many more. We have developed a range of applications across these industries.

Our development models

You can select the development models which is best for you. We have the following options.

Offshore Projects

Work is carried out on a project by project basis. We architect, design, develop, maintain and support a project/product for our customers.

Dedicated Offshore Teams

A dedicated team is retained for a particular client. This is relevant for organizations doing ongoing product development. There is a minimum engagement period for the team.

On-site Consultants

We can send our technical experts to work on site at the client location for a specified period of time. Costing for this will be determined on a case by case basis.
