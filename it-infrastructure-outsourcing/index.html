IT Infrastructure Outsourcing

Businesses with little or no in-house IT resources find it more economical and efficient to outsource the administration of their IT infrastructure to MB9. Our managed IT plan offers regular maintenance and advice to ensure that your systems are operating optimally. Our IT Outsourcing Services include monitoring of your servers and office network.  We also help companies set up their IT infrastructure that allows for easy scalability as their business grows.

The IT Infrastructure team provides a one-stop shop for our clients’ IT needs, from procurement, project implementation to technical support. Our team of engineers is trained to provide advice on your office  network infrastructure requirements and resources to ensure your entire IT system set-up brings optimal benefit to your business.  With us as your Virtual IT Department, you have peace of mind knowing that your IT infrastructure is in trusted hands. You also enjoy lower IT service costs and reduced downtime, as well as a secure business platform.

When should one outsource?
  • When you are starting up and unsure what IT resources you will need
  • When your non IT personnel are spending more than a quarter of their time on IT Support
  • When there is slow network or Internet access in your office
  • When you are concerned over the confidentiality and integrity of your data
  • When you suffer loss in productivity as a result of virus or spam attacks
