Brand MB9

Innovation, Trust, Execution represents MB9s core values.
  • Innovation: We apply Innovative and Creative thinking to provide best solution.
  • Trust: We follow the principles of trust in all our dealings - A focus on customer, willingness to work together, long term relationship perspective and being fully transparent in our dealings.
  • Execution: We emphasize on clarity on our commitments to the customers and are accountable. We make sure we deliver our commitment to the utmost satisfaction



The MB9 logo is set in bold upper case, portraying a bold young company with great growth potential and strong in character. The use of upper case font style reflects the company’s strong foundation. The color of dark blue is often associated with importance, confidence, power, intelligence, that goes well to define the spirit of the company.

The sharp dash in M indicates the speed and agility. The flower (*) denotes power, the power to innovate, the power of trust we place in all relationship, and the engineering and execution power that we posses.
