3DSocial

3DSocial is about empowering  by engaging communities. For corporates our solutions help in better customer engagement, effective marketing and intelligent data analysis.

Benefits

3DSocial benefits your business by supercharging your marketing and customer relations. The following are some of the benefits using 3DSocial.

Stay Updated On your Business Domain News

3DSocial lets you stay in touch with the latest happenings in your domain. We collect data from various sources and give it to you. This can be data about your company or the industry you work in - including how customers perceive your company.

Attract More Web-Users by Dynamic Content

We provide you industry related content, which you can publish or link to. This will make your users will spend more time on your site. Also, you can attract new visitors by being an industry insider, who can be your potential customers.

Get Informed about news on your Company

We gather customer perception about your company from various online sources. By publishing and linking to positive reviews and opinions by the online community, your company is able to increase the marketing momentum. Get news from popular sources, where there is mention about your business/product/website. Display them on your own website.

Highly Effective online marketing Strategy

Get Repeated Online visitors to your website, by displaying relevant and interesting content from news, blogs and tweets. Self advertising cost in your own site is zero. This will improve your SEO and get Search Engine visitors which can convert into your customers.

Continuous Customer engagement

Logged in user credentials can be used for targeted marketing. The user comments/discussion helps you keep in touch with users in a better way.

Feedback system

3DSocial includes a module to get feedback from users on your product/service. Communicate with them from your website, rather than from other 3'rd party websites!
