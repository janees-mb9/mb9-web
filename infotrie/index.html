InfoTrie - Competitor Tracking Solutions.

InfoTrie - Competitor Tracking allows companies to track their competition and the industry happenings.

Benefits

  • Research and Monitoring

    Marketers, Analysts and Researchers use InfoTrie to stay updated on their industry and product trends.

  • Competitor Tracking

    Get to know of strategic moves by the competition before it is too late.

How it Works

InfoTrie automatically does content aggregation from News Sources, Blogs and Social Networks. It arranges the Contextually Related Articles, Images and Videos into Topics. InfoTrie tracks competitor moves and provides this information in a timely manner.
