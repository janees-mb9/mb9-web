Trainer

CKK

Kien Kah has over 20years experience in capital markets, specializing in Fixed Income. He started his career in Citibank Singapore in 1996 as a Treasury Management Associate and was subsequently posted to the G7 Money Market desk where he worked as a dealer for 4 years.

After a 2.5year stint in the Regional Treasury’s e-Commerce unit, Kien Kah was assigned to the Financial Institutional Sales Team, dealing in SGS and SGD Corporate Bonds and G7 Rates.

Kien Kah joined JP Morgan Singapore in 2005 as part of the Credit and Rates Sales Team covering South-East Asian Central Banks / Sovereign Wealth Funds and Financial Institutional clients.  Product coverage was focused on USD and EUR Rates and Credit – with a focus on the Asian Corporate Bond market.  He dealt in US and European asset-backed securities, US mortgage-backed securities, US Collateralized Mortgage Obligations, USD and EUR denominated corporate bond and all forms of credit derivatives (CDS, ITRAXX and CDX credit indices).  He made Executive Director in 2009 and experienced first-hand the Credit Crunch in 2007-8 and the European Debt Crisis during his 7.5year stint in JP Morgan.

More recently, Kien Kah joined MUFJ Securities in 2013, where he spearheaded MUFJ’s expansion into the non-JPY rates business.  His product focus was on USD and JPY rates, and repo.
