Software R&D Consulting

Software Consulting Services offered

We offer the following services for our clients.

Algorithm Design, Implementation and Optimization

MB9 have very good experience in algorithm design and optimization. We can help you choose the best algorithms for a task and implement it for you. Our R&D services include conducting research, feasibility analysis and implementation of large algorithm-intensive software projects.

Product Requirement to Rapid Prototype

We take you through the customer’s initial vague idea to a fast prototype implementation. This service can help customer to get a feel of his product as an initial working prototype and visualize his ideas and improve his ideas at an early stage. Startups or mid-sized companies looking to get into a new product, but doesn't have the resources and bandwidth to implement it in-house can leverage this service. After the prototype is done, it can be developed further with MB9 or in-house at the customer’s facility.

Product Development Partner

MB9 can take your product ideas to finished product. We provide end-to-end service from Software Architecture to Design to Implementation to the Finished product.

Custom software solution R&D

MB9 can develop your requirements for custom software for your business uses. We can perform research, feasibility analysis and design for your requirements and implementation and implement it as finished product.
