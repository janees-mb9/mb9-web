End to End Solution

In general there are two kind of iPhone Apps.

  1. Standalone apps, which are fully contained inside phone

If your app requires to pull content from websites, supports push notification or does synchronization then you require an interactive app. Interactive apps have server side components. These apps should have intuitive design and fast server response time .
