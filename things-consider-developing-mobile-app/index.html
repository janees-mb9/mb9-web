Things to consider before developing a Mobile App

What are the goals for developing the App ?

Before embarking on mobile app development, the goals and motives should be very clear. Well defined goals will help you in arriving at decisions at each step of app development process. The motive could be improve your Brand impressions, increase business sales, get more leads, sell more apps or simply provide a service.

Have you done any market research ?

Segment the market to determine who will benefit from your app. Identify the needs of this target segment. Ask them questions. Based on feedback refine your idea for app. Cut features that are not relevant.

What is your marketing plan ?

Having determined the target segment, you have to find ways to reach out to them. Determine the tactics to achieve goals for app marketing. Find your app promotion and distribution channels

How the app is going to be used ?

Study the target segment and find use cases on how the app is going to be used. The app features and design should be specifically aligned to these use scenarios. For instance, if you have an application that is going to be used while walking around , then a geo-location feature would come handy.

Your Budget

Its a common misconception that a restricted budget could constrain your app development. At MB9 we advise all our clients to have a balance between the app features and budget, without compromising the quality. With a limited budget, you should be looking at a long term engagement, where your app is developed in stages.

Long Term Commitment

To build a successful app requires commitment and patience. You should be ready to put effort in reaching out to your audience, understand their feedback and incorporate those in continuous app updates. We relish at having long lasting relationship with clients.
