AusInNews - Australia News in Topics for WP7

AusInNews - WP7 is a Windows Phone 7 app which helps you track news from Australia. The app monitors all newspapers from the region and presents news grouped in topics.

App Description

AusInNews gathers and organizes news from various Australia based newspapers in real-time. It groups news into topics and gives you a concise view of what is happening across Australia. By using AusInNews app, you are able to easily keep track of the news and trends collected for you right from your phone.
