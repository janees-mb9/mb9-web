MB9 provides customized and highly differentiated financial training programs for professionals working in banks, insurance, financial institutions, corporates and legal services. We identify the relevant courses that can improve the skills and knowledge gaps for our clients, targeting to increase the level of financial market sophistication, thereby raising the degree of investment interaction and decision making with their clients.

KEY DIFFERENTIATING FACTORS IN OUR PROGRAMS

Only highly experienced professionals with at least 20years industry exposure.

capital markets’ development and issues, real-time case studies and application from both institutional investors and

Putting our reputation on the line with our explicit investment opinions

Our experienced lecturers are encouraged to share their investment outlook and opinions on specific securities and the wider market. This sets us apart as no other course trainers would put their reputation on the line by giving a view, backing it up with fundamental reasons, and most importantly, following through with specific entry and exit strategies.

Wholistic program structure to understand investment strategies and navigate myriad of financial products

Value-add in sharing best practice Investment Techniques and Risk Management by leading hedge fund managers and institutional portfolio managers

Deeper understanding of financial markets = higher level of client sophistication and interaction
