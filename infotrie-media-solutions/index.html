InfoTrie - Media Solutions

InfoTrie - Media Solutions enable media houses to gather and organize content from their own archives or from the web.

Benefits for Publishers

  • Enhanced Editorial Experience

    Publishers use InfoTrie to enhance the editorial experience of their website by integrating with their archives. It helps editors to get a 360 degree view of the current Topics for presenting the best and tightly focused content.

  • Increase your Reach

    Publishers and Portals use InfoTrie to track information from niche areas, tangentially related to their core areas of interest, increasing their reach.

  • Improve Web Traffic and Revenue

    Publishers use InfoTrie to increase their organic traffic, subscriber acquisition, SEO and for better search engine page rank.

How it Works

InfoTrie automatically does content aggregation from News Sources, Blogs and Social Networks. It arranges the Contextually Related Articles, Images and Videos into Topics. InfoTrie can create Topic Pages on categories ranging from Politics, Finance, Sports, Country/Region specific Topics or niche domain Topics like Pharmaceutical etc.

  1. You choose the domain, industry, demography and type of service.
  2. We tune InfoTrie engine to your requirements.
  3. We provide you with the option of complete integration into your existing web or intranet application through API’s. We can also provide a custom, fully hosted and managed platform which can be used for reaching out to your users.

Industry Specific Solutions

News

InfoTrie can do a Country or Region Specific news Topic aggregation. InfoTrie Topic aggregation is available for US, UK , Australia, Middle East, Asia Pacific and India. It can be automatically set up for any other country or region of choice.

Finance

InfoTrie can track Finance topics based on region, country or categories like forex , commodities , insurance , investment banking etc . It allows to filter topics into Bearish or Bullish trends. It also tracks topics around Stock Portfolios.  

Sports

InfoTrie tracks news topics from various sports. It includes soccer, cricket, tennis, golf and more. It can track topics around various sporting events.

Technology

InfoTrie can get the latest trending topics in technology. It does so by tracking the various tech blogs, forums and news sources. 

Travel

InfoTrie can track the topics relevant for travellers and travel industry. It aggregates information from various regional and international news sources, blogs and social networks.

Automobiles

InfoTrie can get the latest topics , news and reviews on the latest Car Models. It aggregates information on the popular brands and presents and overview on the trends in this industry
